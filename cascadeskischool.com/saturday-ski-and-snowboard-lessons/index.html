Saturday Ski & Snowboard Lessons

(Programs #1-6)

7 weeks, beginning January 10, 2026

No classes will be held on Saturday, February 14 due to the President’s Day Holiday.

All classes are held from 9:45am-12:15pm

Program 1 – Snow Sprouts – Skiing for Ages 4 and 5   – PARENT PARTICIPATION REQUIRED 

Must be 4 years old prior to 1/1/26

Sow the seeds of winter fun with our program designed for young children. Parents or guardians join the small class of up to 5 students and are part of the learning process. Our instructors provide a safe, consistent learning environment and age-appropriate lessons for your child.  Parents/guardians need to participate in the lesson and be prepared to ride the lift with their child when the class is ready.

1 Class per day (2.5 hours)………………………………………….$675

 

Program 2 – Super Snow Sprouts – Skiing for Ages 5 and 6 – PARENT PARTICIPATION REQUIRED

The second part of our Snow Sprouts program continues for the “seasoned” skier. This class is for the student who can participate for the duration of the class with less involvement from parent or guardian. Small class size of up to 5 students.  Parents/guardians need to participate in the lesson and be prepared to ride the lift with their child.

1 Class per day (2.5 hours)…………………………………….$675
